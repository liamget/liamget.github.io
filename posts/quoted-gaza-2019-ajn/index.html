Quoted: Escalation in Israel & Gaza

10 May 2019

I was quoted in the Australian Jewish News about the recent escalation in violence in Israel & Gaza:

We stand with our many partners and grantees who are working for a future in which no parent or child, in the south of Israel or in Gaza, will know war.

There is no justification for Hamas rockets fired indiscriminately toward civilians. And the Israeli government will not bring about a peaceful future by escalating a military operation in Gaza; only a long-term agreement will end the rockets and address the humanitarian situation. A peaceful future cannot be built on war.

We are with the families of the innocent people who have been killed or harmed.
